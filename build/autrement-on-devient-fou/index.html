Céline Foulon.

Original Language: French
Subtitles: English, German

ABOUT THE INTERVIEW WITH HENRI LAVRILLAT:

Henri Lavrillat’s memories around Second World War, basically the Compulsory Work Service “STO” (Service du Travail Obligatoire) first on Cap Ferret and then in Bordeaux. He talks about how the bunkers in Cap Ferret - I have been taking photos of for my project "Disappearance" since 2005 - were constructed; he refers to my work in regard to his history on site, the state of the bunkers in the "here and now" and of course how it all started. (Foukauld-La Disparition).

Villeneuve-sur-lot, March 14th 2012
