BAKARY J / 2006

Vienna 2006
C-Prints: 37,5 cm x 29,2 cm to 150 cm x 120 cm
Edition: 3+2

The photography section is getting shaped and reorganized. I am sorry for the inconveniences.

ABOUT THE PROJECT:

One of my photographic excursions lead me into an abandonned depot in vienna. I took a photo of a stickman, whose face - originally on a board on the wall - was lying on the floor; scrunched. Some weeks after that the asylum seeker Bakary J. was abused and nearly beaten to death by an alien branch of the austrian police in that same building.
