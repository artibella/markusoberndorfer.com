SWIM AT OWN RISK / 2006

Miami 2006
C-Prints: 37,5 cm x 29,2 cm to 150 cm x 120 cm
Edition: 3+2

The photography section is getting shaped and reorganized. I am sorry for the inconveniences.

WHAT I EXPECT FROM MY PHOTOGRAPHY:

Local conditions, surroundings and snapshots around a photoshooting in Miami with Jork Weismann. On and inbetween location; places otherwise difficult to access but allow different views on a city and its individual parallel worlds.

"Swim at Own Risk" stands as a metaphor for the different worlds we live in. No matter who, no matter how, no matter where ... everyone has to find its own way to navigate through the hurdles.
