ABOUT THE PROJECT:

Lenscapes #02 vinylpostcard is supposed to provide the soundtrack to photograph "Room #106" shown together with the portable turntable in the installation. The soundtrack shall help the spectator to plunge into the image "Room #106" and screen a film to it in front to his/her inner eye. So to say: the soundtrack to an in any case virtually moved photograph. (more about actually and virtually moved images in Lydia Nsiah's book "Hybrid: Fotofilm")
