BLITZKRIEG / 2009

1min 02sec, Vienna, 2009
Starring: Anker, Busk & emilone
Original: 1920 x 1440 px
© Markus Oberndorfer 2009

Installation of 25 disco flashlights
by Anker, Busk & emilone
© by the artists

ABOUT THE PROJECT”:

"The matrix of a city is split into snapshots and resolved into something new. Breaking this existing order "Graffiti" manages to irritate for an instant, although non of these traces last forever ... [...]

[...] A project by Anker, Busk and emilone for the exhibition „Krieg der Zeichen“ ("War of symbols/signs" at designforum MQW 2009). Programmed by Manuel Posch. Light enginieering von Piotr Znajkowski"
